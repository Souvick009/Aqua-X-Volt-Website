FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

1. Why the bot is not responding ?

There are 2 possible reasons why the bot is not respondong to you -:
1. You don't have permissions in the server and Errormsg mode is turned off in the server.
2. Check the permissions of Aqua X Volt.
3. Bot is offline. You can check the bot status by viewing its profile or joining our server.

2. How to change the prefix ?

To change the prefix of the bot, you can run the command =settings setprefix [prefix of your choice]. [ ] is not required.

3. I just noticed that there are some bugs in the bot, it's not working properly, How can I fix it?

You can join our official server and report all the bugs in the #bugs-report channel

4. How can i get to know the current status of Aqua x Volt ?

You can join our official server and check the channel named #bot-status to know the current bot status.

5. I forgot the prefix of AxV. How can i know what's the current prefix of the bot ?

To know the prefix of the bot, you can mention the bot and use the help command.
Example:- @Aqua X Volt#6142 help
Prefix will be shown in the embed sent by the bot.

6. People keep trying to use the bot even when they don't have permissions and bot responds with the permission error message everytime. This basically spams my server, so how can I stop that ?

You can disable errormsg mode, its enabled my default. On disabling it, bot won't respond to people who don't have permissions in your server.
